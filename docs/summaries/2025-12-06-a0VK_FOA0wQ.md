# 全新海戰重製解析

## 重點摘要
遊戲的「海戰重製」更新引入了新單位「浩克」（Hulk），改變了核心海軍的「三位一體」組合，並將升級項目移至大學，最終帶來一個重大利好：將船塢介面簡化為單一頁面。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開頭，主講人宣布他將要介紹全新的「海戰重製」內容，並為不清楚的觀眾快速回顧改動了什麼。
- **中段**：主講人詳細列舉了更新的具體內容：新增了近戰單位「浩克」、帝王時代新增了「戰艦」升級、核心單位組合從「槳帆戰船、火戰船、爆破船」改為「槳帆戰船、火戰船、浩克」。同時，爆破船獲得了獨立的升級路線，所有其他船艦升級都被移到了大學，並重調了數值。
- **結尾**：主講人點出這次重製帶來的一個巨大優點，就是船塢（Dock）的操作介面被清理乾淨，現在所有東西都在一個頁面上，他認為這「非常巨大」。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **新單位改變戰術**：新增的近戰單位「浩克」取代了爆破船在核心組合中的地位，改變了海戰的基本戰術。
2.  **升級路徑集中化**：將大部分船艦升級從船塢移至大學，簡化了建築分工，讓玩家能更直觀地找到升級選項。
3.  **UI/UX 大幅優化**：最重要的成果是將船塢介面簡化成單一頁面，這是一項巨大的使用者體驗改善。
4.  **漁船生存能力提升**：漁船現在可以駐紮在船塢中，為經濟單位提供了更好的保護。
5.  **舊單位獲得新定位**：爆破船（Demo）不再是核心三角的一員，但擁有了獨立的城堡與帝王時代升級，使其成為一個更具針對性的選擇。

# Analysis of the New Water Rework

## TL;DR
The water rework introduces the new "Hulk" unit, changes the core naval trio, and moves upgrades to the University. The biggest benefit is a vastly simplified, one-page user interface for the Dock.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The speaker introduces the topic: the new water rework. He states he will provide a quick recap of the changes for those who are not up to date.
- **Middle**: The speaker details the specific changes: the addition of the Hulk (a melee unit), a new core trio of Galley/Fires/Hulk, separate upgrades for Demolition ships, the relocation of other ship upgrades to the university, and number adjustments for ships. Fishing ships can also now garrison in Docks.
- **End**: The speaker concludes by highlighting the single biggest benefit of the rework: it cleans up the Dock interface into a single page, which he emphasizes is a "huge" improvement.

## Key Insights
1.  **New Unit Changes Tactics**: The introduction of the Hulk, a melee water unit, replaces the Demolition ship in the core unit composition, shifting fundamental naval strategy.
2.  **Centralized Upgrade Path**: Moving most ship upgrades from the Dock to the University centralizes the tech tree and makes finding upgrades more intuitive.
3.  **Major UI/UX Improvement**: The most significant outcome is the simplification of the Dock interface to a single page, which is a massive quality-of-life enhancement.
4.  **Improved Fishing Ship Survivability**: Fishing ships can now garrison inside docks, offering better protection for economic units.
5.  **Old Units Get a New Role**: The Demolition ship is no longer part of the core trio but now has its own dedicated upgrades, making it more of a specialized counter unit.
