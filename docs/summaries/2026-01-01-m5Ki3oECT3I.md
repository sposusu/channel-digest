# 世紀帝國2：職業選手帶領400分新手隊友的史詩級逆轉勝

## 重點摘要
在《世紀帝國2》的一場高壓排名賽中，職業選手 Hera 保護並指導一位僅有400分的菜鳥隊友 Mahmood。Hera 採用了保護性的「肉盾」策略，並在遊戲後期發動了毀滅性的「詩文塔」攻勢，最終帶領團隊奇蹟般地戰勝了實力強大的對手。

## 故事大綱
- **開場：** 職業選手 Hera 在一場團隊排名賽中，與一位名叫 Mahmood 的491分（ELO）低分段玩家配對。意識到隊友是個新手後，Hera 決定承擔起保護他的責任，並指導他前往地圖「朝聖者」的一個安全角落建立基地，避免過早與敵人交戰。
- **中段：** Hera 扮演著 Mahmood 的「肉盾」，在他的前方建立防線。他幽默地向 Mahmood 徵收綿羊作為「皇家保護稅」，但也給予他資源和鼓勵。Hera 快速發展經濟，成功奪取了水域控制權，並利用維京人的獨特科技「詩文塔」為遊戲後期佈局。期間，另一位隊友 Krons 的基地被摧毀，但 Hera 鼓勵他不要放棄，並為他提供資源，保住了團隊的希望。
- **結尾：** 當敵方發動總攻時，Hera 的「詩文塔」陣線發揮了奇效。這些塔樓能造成穿透傷害，敵軍在他的防線面前瞬間瓦解。面對這道無法逾越的防線和持續的壓力，敵方隊伍最終選擇投降，Hera、Mahmood 和團隊贏得了一場看似不可能的勝利。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **保護新手隊友是致勝關鍵：** 與其責怪低分段隊友，不如成為他的「肉盾」，為他創造安全的發育空間，讓他也能為團隊做出貢獻。
2.  **ELO並不能代表一切：** 儘管 Mahmood 分數很低，但他聽從指揮並做出了一些亮眼操作（如早期建造碼頭），證明了新手玩家也能成為可靠的盟友。
3.  **非常規戰術的威力：** 遊戲後期的「塔攻」戰術，尤其是維京的詩文塔，可以成為扭轉戰局的毀滅性武器。
4.  **永不放棄的精神：** 隊友 Krons 雖然失去了基地，但他堅持沒有退出遊戲，這為團隊保留了勝利的火種，避免了因人數劣勢而導致的崩盤。
5.  **戰略性地圖控制：** 在像「朝聖者」這樣複雜的地圖上，佔據一個易守難攻的角落，比直接衝向資源豐富的中心地帶更為明智。

# Age of Empires 2: Pro Player Carries 400 ELO Newbie to a Legendary Ranked Win

## TL;DR
In a high-stakes ranked match of *Age of Empires II*, pro player Hera protects and guides a 400-ELO beginner, Mahmood. By adopting a "meat shield" strategy and unleashing a devastating late-game "Sven Tower" push, Hera leads the underdog team to a miraculous victory against formidable opponents.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning:** Pro player Hera gets matched with Mahmood, a 491 ELO player, in a team ranked game. Realizing his teammate is a novice, Hera decides to take on the role of his protector, guiding him to a safe corner of the "Pilgrims" map to avoid early conflict.
- **Middle:** Hera acts as Mahmood's "meat shield," setting up defensively in front of him. He humorously "taxes" Mahmood's sheep for "royal protection" while also giving him resources and encouragement. Hera builds his economy, wins water control, and prepares for a late-game strategy centered on the Viking Unique Tech, "Sven Towers." Meanwhile, another teammate, Krons, loses his base, but Hera encourages him to stay in the game and sends resources, preserving the team's chances.
- **End:** As the enemy launches a major attack, Hera's line of Sven Towers proves its immense power. The towers, which deal pass-through damage, melt the enemy army. Faced with this impenetrable defense and relentless pressure, the enemy team resigns, securing an improbable victory for Hera, Mahmood, and the team.

## Key Insights
1.  **Protecting Weaker Teammates Wins Games:** Instead of blaming a low-ELO teammate, acting as their "meat shield" to create a safe space for them to develop can enable them to contribute effectively.
2.  **ELO Isn't Everything:** Despite his low rating, Mahmood followed instructions and made smart plays (like an early dock), proving that new players can be valuable allies.
3.  **The Power of Unconventional Strategies:** A late-game tower push, particularly with the devastating Sven Towers of the Vikings, can be an incredibly powerful and game-deciding tactic.
4.  **Resilience is Crucial:** Teammate Krons didn't quit even after losing his base. By staying in the game, he kept the team at full strength, which was vital for the eventual comeback.
5.  **Strategic Map Positioning:** On a complex map like Pilgrims, securing a defensible corner is often a wiser strategy than rushing to the resource-rich, contested center.
