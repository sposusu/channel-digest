# 華碩 ROG XBOX Handheld X 掌機評測：巨幅能效提升

## 重點摘要
全新的 ROG XBOX Handheld X 透過搭載特製的 AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme 處理器，實現了革命性的能效躍進。它能在顯著降低的功耗下提供與前代高功耗模式相當的遊戲性能，從而帶來更長的電池續航和更安靜的遊戲體驗，並結合更舒適的握持手感與深度整合的 Xbox 軟體，成為一台極具競爭力的 Windows 掌機。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開箱並介紹了號稱「首款純血 Xbox 掌機」的 ROG XBOX Handheld X。主持人將其與前代 ROG Ally 及 Steam Deck 進行尺寸比較，並強調雖然重量略增，但全新設計的握持角度讓手感舒適度大幅提升。

- **中段**：評測深入探討了核心升級。首先是軟體層面，開機即進入的 Xbox 全螢幕體驗模式，整合了多平台遊戲庫，使其更像一台專用遊戲機。接著，拆機揭示了硬體奧秘：更高效的散熱系統，以及最重要的核心——AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme 處理器。影片解釋了這款晶片是透過遮蔽部分核心，讓遊戲在有限功耗下獲得更高頻率，從而提升能效。遊戲實測（如《極限競速：地平線 5》和《黑神話：悟空》）數據證明，它僅用 17W 功耗就能達到前代 25W-30W 的性能水準。

- **結尾**：影片以電池續航力測試收尾，結果顯示在同等性能下，AAA 遊戲續航時間大幅延長了近 45 分鐘。主持人總結認為，這款掌機在手感、散熱、性能和噪音控制上都有顯著進步，尤其是能效和握感堪稱市場頂尖。最後公布其售價為 6,499 人民幣。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **能效是最大亮點**：Z2 Extreme 處理器的核心優勢在於驚人的能效比，能用更低的功耗（如 17W）達成前代需要更高功耗（25W+）才能實現的遊戲幀率。
2.  **中低功耗性能質變**：這一代掌機在 13W-17W 的功耗區間性能表現大幅優化，讓 AAA 遊戲在不插電情況下的實用性顯著增強，並直接延長了續航。
3.  **軟硬體深度整合**：全新的 Xbox 體驗模式和控制中心，讓它擺脫了普通 Windows PC 的操作感，更趨近於一台無縫的專用遊戲主機。
4.  **晶片設計的巧思**：透過減少 CPU 核心數以換取在功耗限制下更高的單核頻率，是一種針對遊戲負載的聰明優化策略。同時，保留的 NPU 也為未來的 Windows AI 功能埋下伏筆。
5.  **體驗細節全面提升**：除了性能，更舒適的握持手感和明顯更安靜的風扇噪音，共同打造了遠超前代的綜合使用體驗。

## 精彩時刻
- **揭示核心秘密**：當影片中拆下散熱器，揭示 Z2 Extreme 處理器其實是 HX370 晶片的「特供版」時，展現了其硬體設計的獨到之處。
- **驚人的能效數據**：在 3DMARK 測試中，Z2E 在 25W 主機板功耗下，GPU 性能就追平了前代 Z1E 在 50W 功耗下的表現。
- **《黑神話：悟空》續航測試**：在同樣 60 幀的流暢體驗下，新款掌機續航達到 2 小時 37 分鐘，相比前代（需更高功耗）的 1 小時 54 分鐘，提升巨大。
- **對握持手感的盛讚**：「在握持舒適度上，我認為市面上沒有其他對手能與之匹敵。」

# ASUS ROG XBOX Handheld X Review: A Huge Leap in Efficiency

## TL;DR
The new ROG XBOX Handheld X achieves a revolutionary leap in energy efficiency with its custom AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme processor. It delivers gaming performance comparable to the previous generation's high-power modes but at significantly lower wattages, resulting in longer battery life, a quieter gaming experience, a more comfortable grip, and deep Xbox software integration, making it a highly competitive Windows handheld.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The video unboxes and introduces the ROG XBOX Handheld X, billed as the "first pure Xbox handheld." The host compares its size to the previous ROG Ally and Steam Deck, highlighting that despite a slight weight increase, the new grip angle makes it much more comfortable to hold.

- **Middle**: The review dives deep into the core upgrades. First is the software: a new Xbox "Full Screen Experience Mode" on startup integrates multiple game libraries, making it feel more like a dedicated console. Then, a teardown reveals the hardware secrets: a more efficient cooling system and, most importantly, the AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme processor. The video explains this chip is a modified die that disables some cores to allow others to run at higher frequencies under a limited power budget, boosting efficiency. In-game benchmarks (like *Forza Horizon 5* and *Black Myth: Wukong*) prove this, showing it can match the performance of the previous generation's 25W-30W modes using only 17W.

- **End**: The video concludes with a battery life test, which shows a substantial increase of nearly 45 minutes for AAA gaming at the same performance level. The host concludes that the device has made significant improvements in feel, cooling, performance, and noise, with its efficiency and grip being top-of-the-line. The price is announced as 6,499 RMB.

## Key Insights
1.  **Efficiency is the Main Highlight**: The Z2 Extreme processor's key advantage is its incredible performance-per-watt, achieving the same frame rates as the previous generation at a much lower power draw (e.g., 17W vs. 25W+).
2.  **A Breakthrough in Low-Power Performance**: The device's performance at lower power settings (13W-17W) has been drastically improved, making unplugged AAA gaming far more practical and directly extending battery life.
3.  **Deep Hardware-Software Integration**: The new Xbox experience mode and control center move it away from the feel of a standard Windows PC and closer to a seamless, dedicated gaming console.
4.  **Clever Chip Design**: Reducing CPU cores to achieve higher single-core frequencies under a power limit is a smart optimization strategy specifically for gaming workloads. The retained NPU also prepares it for future Windows AI features.
5.  **A Better Overall Experience**: Beyond performance, the more comfortable grip and noticeably quieter fan create a comprehensive user experience that is far superior to its predecessor.

## Notable Moments
- **Revealing the Core Secret**: The moment the video reveals the Z2 Extreme processor is a special version of the HX370 die, showcasing its unique hardware design.
- **Stunning Efficiency Numbers**: In 3DMARK tests, the Z2E's GPU at 25W of board power matched the performance of the previous-gen Z1E at 50W.
- **The *Black Myth: Wukong* Battery Test**: Achieving 2 hours and 37 minutes of runtime at a smooth 60fps, a massive improvement over the previous gen's 1 hour and 54 minutes (which required higher power).
- **High Praise for the Grip**: "When it comes to grip comfort, I don't think there's anything else on the market that can rival it."
