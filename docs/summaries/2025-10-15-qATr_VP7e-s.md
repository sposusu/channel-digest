# 首款「純血Xbox掌機」？ROG XBOX掌機X上手評測

## 重點摘要
ROG與Xbox合作推出的新款掌機「ROG XBOX Handheld X」，憑藉其大幅優化的AMD Z2 Extreme處理器，在中低功耗下實現了卓越的能效和續航力。結合深度整合的Xbox系統體驗與無可匹敵的握持手感，使其成為一台極具競爭力的Windows遊戲掌機。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開箱並介紹了號稱首款「純血Xbox掌機」的ROG XBOX Handheld X。與前代及Steam Deck尺寸相仿，但透過調整機身角度，大幅提升了人體工學設計，握持感極為舒適。
- **中段**：深入探討了幾大核心升級。首先是系統層面，開機即進入Xbox全螢幕體驗模式，整合了多平台遊戲庫，操作流暢。其次是硬體，拆解後展示了更安靜、高效的散熱模組，並揭示了核心處理器AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme的秘密——它是為掌機低功耗場景特別優化的Zen5架構晶片。遊戲實測（《極限競速：地平線5》、《黑神話：悟空》）證明，它在17W功耗下的表現能媲美甚至超越前代25W-30W的性能。
- **結尾**：影片總結了能效提升帶來的顯著續航優勢：AAA遊戲續航時間增加了約40分鐘，而2D遊戲則延長了超過一小時。評測者稱讚其在手感、散熱、性能和噪音方面的全面進步，認為其綜合體驗非常強大。最後公布其首發價為6,499人民幣。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **能效是最大亮點**：新的Z2 Extreme處理器是關鍵，它讓掌機在17W的「甜蜜點」功耗下，就能達到前代需要25W-30W才能實現的遊戲性能，從而直接轉化為更長的電池續航。
2.  **真正的類主機體驗**：系統級的Xbox UI深度整合，讓它擺脫了普通Windows PC的繁瑣感，使用起來更像一台專門的遊戲主機。
3.  **人體工學的勝利**：儘管重量略有增加，但極度舒適的握持感被評為「市面無可匹敵」，讓長時間遊玩不易疲勞。
4.  **更安靜的遊戲體驗**：全新的散熱風扇設計，在提升散熱效率的同時，也將高負載下的噪音從30dB降至27dB，體驗更沉浸。
5.  **低功耗模式不再雞肋**：前代在13W低功耗下性能衰減嚴重，而這一代進行了優化，使其在13W下依然能提供接近可玩的幀率，實用性大增。

## 精彩時刻
- **驚人的能效比**：「在17W的功耗牆下，它就能維持穩定的60幀，而上一代掌機要開到25W功耗才能達到這個17W的表現。」
- **對握持感的極高評價**：「我認為目前市面上沒有其他東西可以與之匹敵，這就是所謂的握持舒適度。」
- **揭示處理器真相**：拆解並解釋Z2 Extreme處理器是基於HX370，透過硬體屏蔽部分核心，使剩餘核心在有限功耗下能運行在更高頻率，專為掌機優化。

# The First "Pure Xbox Handheld"? ROG XBOX Handheld X Review

## TL;DR
The new ROG XBOX Handheld X, a collaboration with Xbox, leverages a highly optimized AMD Z2 Extreme processor to achieve outstanding power efficiency and battery life at low-to-mid power settings. Combined with a deeply integrated Xbox system experience and unrivaled ergonomics, it stands out as a highly competitive Windows gaming handheld.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The video unboxes and introduces the ROG XBOX Handheld X, touted as the first "pure Xbox handheld." Its size is similar to its predecessor and the Steam Deck, but its ergonomics have been significantly improved with adjusted angles, making it extremely comfortable to hold.
- **Middle**: The review delves into several core upgrades. First, on the software side, it boots into a full-screen Xbox experience mode that unifies game libraries from multiple platforms for a seamless experience. Second, a hardware teardown reveals a quieter, more efficient cooling module and the secret behind its core processor, the AMD Ryzen AI Z2 Extreme—a Zen5 architecture chip specially optimized for low-power handheld scenarios. Game tests in *Forza Horizon 5* and *Black Myth: Wukong* prove that its performance at 17W can match or exceed the previous generation's at 25W-30W.
- **End**: The video concludes by highlighting the significant battery life gains from the efficiency improvements: about 40 minutes more for AAA games and over an hour longer for 2D titles. The reviewer praises its comprehensive upgrades in feel, cooling, performance, and noise, deeming it a powerful overall package. The initial price is announced as 6,499 RMB.

## Key Insights
1.  **Efficiency is the Star**: The new Z2 Extreme processor is the key, allowing the handheld to achieve gaming performance at a 17W "sweet spot" that previously required 25W-30W, directly translating to longer battery life.
2.  **A True Console-Like Experience**: Deep, system-level integration of the Xbox UI sheds the clunkiness of a typical Windows PC, making it feel more like a dedicated gaming console.
3.  **An Ergonomic Victory**: Despite a slight weight increase, the incredibly comfortable grip is praised as "unrivaled on the market," reducing fatigue during long gaming sessions.
4.  **A Quieter Gaming Experience**: The new cooling fan design improves thermal efficiency while reducing noise under load from 30dB to a noticeably quieter 27dB, allowing for more immersive gameplay.
5.  **Low-Power Mode is Finally Viable**: While the previous generation's performance dropped off sharply at 13W, this model is optimized to provide near-playable frame rates at that low power level, greatly increasing its versatility.

## Notable Moments
- **Impressive Performance-per-Watt**: "Even when limited to a 17W power consumption wall, it could maintain a stable 60 frames per second. The previous generation of handhelds had to be opened to 25W power consumption to achieve this... performance."
- **High Praise for Comfort**: "I don't think there's anything else on the market that can rival it. That's what it means to be comfortable to hold."
- **Processor Secrets Revealed**: The teardown explains that the Z2 Extreme CPU is based on the HX370 but has specific cores hardware-disabled, allowing the remaining cores to run at higher frequencies within a limited power budget—an optimization tailor-made for handhelds.
