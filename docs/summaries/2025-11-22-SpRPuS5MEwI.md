# M5 Vision Pro 深度評測：地表最強，但值得嗎？

## 重點摘要
新款 M5 Vision Pro 的性能提升，在與升級到最新 VisionOS 26 的舊款 M2 版對比後，顯得微乎其微。真正的明星是軟體更新，它讓舊款設備重獲新生，因此購買價格更低的二手 M2 Vision Pro 成為了遠比購買新款更明智的選擇。

## 故事大綱
- **開場：** 評測者初次體驗 M5 Vision Pro，對其流暢度、手勢辨識精準度和清晰度感到驚艷，認為 M5 晶片帶來了「脫胎換骨」的升級。但當他拿出舊款 M2 版並升級到相同的 VisionOS 26 系統後，才發現所有優化都來自於軟體，兩台設備的體驗幾乎無法分辨。
- **中段：** 影片詳細分析了 M5 版的「升級」。晶片性能雖翻倍，但因應用程式都基於 M2 開發，實際感受不到差異。120Hz 螢幕更新率在絕大多數場景下被限制在 90-100fps，無法發揮。唯一真正有用的硬體升級是全新設計的雙環編織頭帶，舒適度更高。接著，影片探討了 Vision Pro 當前的三大用途：用於「炫耀」的沉浸式 App（如 VR 紀錄片、Voyager 航空追蹤）、作為個人大螢幕影院（特別是搭配 Infuse）、以及作為 Mac 的虛擬螢幕（但仍受限於 60Hz 畫面更新率）。
- **結尾：** 結論是 M5 Vision Pro 是一個很酷但完全沒必要的升級。評測者強烈建議，對此產品感興趣的消費者應該購買二手 M2 版本，因為它在 VisionOS 26 的加持下，能以不到一半的價格提供幾乎相同的頂級體驗。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **軟體大於硬體：** VisionOS 26 系統更新是真正的遊戲規則改變者，它讓舊款 M2 設備的體驗煥然一新，其價值遠超 M5 晶片。
2.  **「紙上談兵」的升級：** M5 晶片近乎翻倍的性能和 120Hz 的螢幕更新率，聽起來很強大，但在實際應用中幾乎無法被用戶感知到。
3.  **二手 M2 版是最佳選擇：** 對於想體驗 Vision Pro 的人來說，購買一台價格腰斬的二手 M2 設備是性價比最高的選擇，能獲得與 M5 版 99% 相同的感受。
4.  **核心用途明確：** Vision Pro 目前最實用的場景是沉浸式影音娛樂和作為 Mac 的外接螢幕，而非遊戲或獨立的辦公生產力工具。
5.  **舒適度是唯一亮點：** 新款的雙環編織頭帶是 M5 版唯一真正實用且有感的硬體改進。

## 精彩時刻
- **「破案時刻」：** 當評測者發現升級系統後的舊款 M2 設備，用起來和新款 M5 一樣流暢時的恍然大悟。
- **航空迷的夢幻 App：** 名為「Voyager」的應用程式，能以 3D 方式即時追蹤全球航班動態，提供「上帝視角」，被譽為航空迷必備。
- **「炫耀型 App」概念：** 形容許多 Vision Pro 應用雖然體驗驚人，但通常只會玩一次給親友看，之後便束之高閣。
- **荒謬的 120Hz：** 吐槽 Vision Pro 擁有 120Hz 螢幕，但在串流 Mac 畫面時卻被限制在 60Hz，令人大失所望。
- **最終忠告：** 「花三萬塊買全新的 M5 版？我覺得就算是發燒友也沒必要浪費這個錢。」

# M5 Vision Pro In-Depth Review: The Best on Earth, But Is It Worth It?

## TL;DR
The new M5 Vision Pro's performance boost is negligible when compared to an older M2 model running the latest VisionOS 26. The software update is the real star, revitalizing the older device and making a cheaper, second-hand M2 Vision Pro a much smarter purchase than the new model.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning:** The reviewer's initial impression of the M5 Vision Pro is overwhelmingly positive, citing its smoothness, precise gesture recognition, and clarity as a "complete rebirth." However, after updating his old M2 model to the same VisionOS 26, he discovers that all the improvements came from the software, making the two devices feel indistinguishable.
- **Middle:** The video details the M5's "upgrades." The chip's performance is nearly doubled, but it's unnoticeable since apps are optimized for the M2. The 120Hz screen refresh rate is capped at 90-100fps in most scenarios. The only truly useful hardware upgrade is the newly designed dual-loop braided headband, which is more comfortable. The video then explores the Vision Pro's current top three uses: "show-off" immersive apps (like VR documentaries and the Voyager flight tracker), serving as a personal big-screen theater (especially with Infuse), and acting as a virtual Mac monitor (though still limited to a 60Hz refresh rate).
- **End:** The conclusion is that the M5 Vision Pro is a cool but entirely unnecessary upgrade. The reviewer strongly recommends that interested consumers buy a used M2 version, which, thanks to VisionOS 26, offers nearly the same premium experience for less than half the price.

## Key Insights
1.  **Software Over Hardware:** The VisionOS 26 update is the real game-changer, making the older M2 device feel brand new and delivering far more value than the M5 chip.
2.  **"On-Paper" Upgrades:** The M5's impressive specs, like its near-double performance and 120Hz screen, sound great but are practically imperceptible in real-world use.
3.  **The Second-Hand M2 is the Best Choice:** For anyone wanting to experience the Vision Pro, buying a used M2 model at a fraction of the original price is the most cost-effective option, delivering 99% of the M5's experience.
4.  **Clear Core Use Cases:** The Vision Pro currently excels at immersive media consumption and as a Mac external display, not as a gaming machine or a standalone productivity tool.
5.  **Comfort is the Only Real Perk:** The new dual-loop braided headband is the only truly meaningful and noticeable hardware improvement in the new model.

## Notable Moments
- **The "Mystery Solved" Moment:** The realization that the old M2 device, once updated, performs just as smoothly as the new M5.
- **Aviation Enthusiast's Dream App:** An app called "Voyager" that provides a 3D, real-time "God's-eye view" of global air traffic, hailed as a must-have for aviation fans.
- **The "Show-Off App" Concept:** Describing many stunning Vision Pro apps as experiences you'll likely only use once to impress friends and family, then never open again.
- **The Absurdity of 120Hz:** Calling out the irony of having a 120Hz display that is still limited to streaming Mac content at 60Hz.
- **The Final Advice:** "Spending 30,000 yuan on a brand new M5 version? I don't think even enthusiasts need to waste that money."
