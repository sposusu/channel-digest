# 挑戰極限：將96核心AMD Threadripper Pro推向1900瓦功耗

## 重點摘要
這部影片展示了AMD Threadripper Pro 9995WX這顆擁有96核心的處理器無與倫比的強大性能。透過極致的超頻與水冷散熱，團隊將其功耗從預設的350瓦推升至驚人的1900瓦，雖然成功榨出了頂尖效能，但也證明了這類硬體是專為極端專業人士設計的，對一般消費者而言既昂貴又不切實際。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開頭就震撼地展示了一台電腦同時運行五個《電馭叛客2077》遊戲實例，並同時進行其他高負載任務，藉此介紹主角——96核心的AMD Threadripper Pro 9995WX CPU。
- **中段**：團隊首先在一塊高階TRX50主機板上進行測試，發現其效能與核心數較少的非Pro版本差距不大。他們意識到，要完全釋放這顆CPU的潛能，必須使用支援八通道記憶體的WRX90主機板。換上正確的主機板、插滿1TB的昂貴記憶體後，他們透過解鎖功耗限制和外接水冷散熱器，將CPU的功耗從350瓦一路推升，最終在Blender渲染測試中達到了恐怖的1900瓦。
- **結尾**：儘管硬體性能達到了巔峰，但在遊戲測試中，《城市：天際線2》因自身優化問題依舊運行不佳，證明了硬體無法拯救軟體。結論是，這顆CPU是一頭性能猛獸，但其天價（僅CPU就超過一萬美元）和複雜性，使其僅適用於需要極致運算能力來節省時間成本的專業工作站或實驗室，而非普通愛好者。

## 關鍵見解
- **極致的功耗與性能**：AMD 9995WX擁有96個核心，透過解鎖功耗限制與極致散熱，可以從350瓦的TDP飆升至超過1900瓦，帶來約10%的額外性能。
- **平台至關重要**：要發揮Threadripper Pro的全部實力，必須搭配支援八通道記憶體的WRX90主機板，僅使用標準的四通道TRX50主機板會造成嚴重的性能瓶頸。
- **記憶體頻寬的影響力**：在7-Zip等對記憶體頻寬敏感的測試中，八通道記憶體的效能是四通道的一倍以上，顯示出記憶體配置對整體性能的巨大影響。
- **硬體無法彌補軟體缺陷**：即使擁有地球上最強大的桌面CPU，也無法讓像《城市：天際線2》這樣優化不良的遊戲流暢運行。
- **專為金字塔頂端打造**：這款處理器及其配套硬體的總成本極其高昂（超過一台汽車），鎖定的客群是那些「時間就是金錢」的專業人士，例如需要進行複雜模擬的大學或工程實驗室。

## 精彩時刻
- 開場同時運行五個《電馭叛客2077》的驚人畫面。
- 當CPU在Blender渲染時功耗衝上1900瓦，遠超1600瓦電源供應器額定功率時，主持人驚呼：「這太嚇人了 (This is terrifying)」。
- 提到當時1TB的RAM價格高達17,000美元，比一台車還貴。
- 主持人開玩笑說，組一台WRX電腦的開銷，讓買一台WRX汽車（Subaru速霸陸）看起來更實際。

# Pushing the 96-Core AMD Threadripper Pro to 1900W

## TL;DR
This video showcases the unparalleled power of the 96-core AMD Threadripper Pro 9995WX CPU. By pushing it to its absolute limits with extreme overclocking and a water chiller, the team boosts its power draw from the default 350W to an astonishing 1900W. While this unlocks peak performance, it also proves that such hardware is incredibly expensive and impractical for consumers, being designed exclusively for top-tier professionals.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The video opens with a stunning demonstration of a single system running five instances of Cyberpunk 2077 simultaneously, alongside other demanding tasks, to introduce the star of the show: the 96-core AMD Threadripper Pro 9995WX CPU.
- **Middle**: The team first tests the CPU on a high-end TRX50 motherboard, finding its performance isn't much better than the non-Pro version with fewer cores. They realize that a WRX90 motherboard with 8-channel memory is required to unlock the CPU's full potential. After switching to the correct board and installing 1TB of expensive RAM, they unlock the power limits and use an external water chiller, pushing the CPU's power draw from 350W all the way to a terrifying 1900W during a Blender render.
- **End**: Despite its peak hardware performance, the CPU still struggles with the poorly optimized game *Cities: Skylines 2*, proving that powerful hardware can't fix bad software. The conclusion is that this CPU is a performance beast, but its astronomical price (over $12,000 for the CPU alone) and complexity make it suitable only for professional workstations or labs where computing time is money, not for enthusiasts.

## Key Insights
- **Extreme Power and Performance**: The AMD 9995WX has 96 cores and can be pushed from its 350W TDP to over 1900W with unlocked power limits and extreme cooling, yielding a ~10% performance boost.
- **The Platform is Crucial**: To leverage the full power of a Threadripper Pro, a WRX90 motherboard with 8-channel memory is essential. Using a standard 4-channel TRX50 board creates a significant performance bottleneck.
- **The Power of Memory Bandwidth**: In memory-sensitive benchmarks like 7-Zip, the 8-channel memory configuration delivered more than double the performance of the 4-channel setup, highlighting its massive impact.
- **Hardware Can't Fix Software**: Even with the most powerful desktop CPU on the planet, a poorly optimized game like *Cities: Skylines 2* will still run poorly.
- **Built for the Apex Tier**: The total cost of this processor and its accompanying hardware is astronomical (more than a car), targeting professionals in fields like university research or engineering labs where "time is money," not consumers.

## Notable Moments
- The mind-blowing opening shot of five instances of Cyberpunk 2077 running at once.
- The host exclaiming, "This is terrifying," as the CPU hits 1900W during a render, far exceeding the 1600W power supply's rating.
- The discussion of the 1TB of RAM costing $17,000 at the time of filming, making it more expensive than a car.
- The joke that the cost of the WRX computer makes a WRX (Subaru) automobile look like the more practical choice.
