# 紅魔11 Pro系列上手：液冷散熱與PC遊戲實測

## 重點摘要
紅魔11 Pro系列搭載最新的8 Elite Gen 5晶片，將手機遊戲性能推向新高度。Pro+版本引入了視覺上很酷的「液冷」散熱系統，而Pro版則配備了驚人的8000mAh電池；實測證明，經過調校後，這款手機能以可玩的幀率運行《黑神話：悟空》等PC大作。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開箱最新的紅魔11 Pro與Pro+，強調其搭載的8E5頂級晶片，並第一時間展示Pro+機身中間獨特的「液冷」通道，可見內部液體流動。
- **中段**：詳細介紹本代升級重點，包含Pro+的液冷系統（實為VC均熱板）、IPX8防水風扇、改善觸控的螢幕，以及兩款機型的電池策略（Pro為8000mAh，Pro+為7500mAh搭配120W快充）。接著進入核心環節：透過轉譯方式在手機上運行多款PC遊戲（如《戰神4》、《黑神話：悟空》、《遠征33》）來壓榨8E5的極限性能，結果顯示幀率比上一代有質的飛躍。
- **結尾**：結論是，8E5的強大性能讓手機以30幀運行PC大作成爲可能，但需要針對不同遊戲進行功耗微調。影片讚揚紅魔在同質化的市場中，持續推出兼具實用性與情感價值的創新設計。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **PC遊戲手機化成真**：8E5晶片的性能強大，已能透過轉譯順暢運行DX11的PC遊戲，在720p解析度下達到30-40幀，是行動遊戲的一大突破。
2.  **極致性能需要調校**：8E5功耗極高，要獲得最佳遊戲體驗，不能無腦全開效能模式，必須針對不同遊戲手動調整CPU與GPU的頻率，以平衡功耗與散熱。
3.  **實用與酷炫的創新**：紅魔持續在電競手機領域探索，從螢幕下鏡頭到這次的液冷通道，這些設計不僅提供了「情感價值」，也確實對遊戲體驗有幫助。
4.  **雙機型定位明確**：Pro版主打8000mAh超大電池，追求極致續航；Pro+則在搭載液冷新技術的同時，提供更快的有線與無線充電。
5.  **補足過往短版**：此代產品升級了IPX8防水風扇和採用新的觸控IC，旨在解決過去被詬病的痛點，提升了日常使用的可靠性。

## 精彩時刻
- **看得見的「液冷」**：Pro+機背上透明的液冷通道，能清楚看到內部液體（含氣泡）流動，視覺效果十足。
- **征服《黑神話：悟空》**：成功在手機上以接近30幀的幀率運行《黑神話：悟空》，相較半年前測試的10幾幀，被評為「質的飛躍」。
- **功耗突破天際**：在運行《遠征33》時，整機功耗一度飆升至18W以上，展現了8E5晶片恐怖的性能釋放。

# Red Magic 11 Pro Series Hands-On: Liquid Cooling & PC Gaming Tested

## TL;DR
The Red Magic 11 Pro series, powered by the latest 8 Elite Gen 5 chip, pushes mobile gaming performance to new heights. The Pro+ model introduces a visually cool "liquid cooling" system, while the Pro version packs a massive 8000mAh battery. Crucially, tests show the phone can now run demanding PC titles like *Black Myth: Wukong* at playable frame rates with proper tuning.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The video unboxes the new Red Magic 11 Pro and Pro+, highlighting their top-tier 8E5 chip and immediately showcasing the unique, visible "liquid cooling" channel on the Pro+'s body.
- **Middle**: It details this generation's key upgrades, including the Pro+'s liquid cooling (which functions like a VC heat spreader), an IPX8 waterproof fan, an improved touch screen, and the different battery strategies for each model (8000mAh for the Pro, 7500mAh with 120W fast charging for the Pro+). The video then moves to its core segment: stress-testing the 8E5's limits by running translated PC games like *God of War 4*, *Black Myth: Wukong*, and *Expedition 33*, revealing a "qualitative leap" in frame rates compared to the previous generation.
- **End**: The conclusion is that the 8E5's power makes running PC masterpieces at 30 fps on a phone viable, though it requires fine-tuning power consumption for each game. The video praises Red Magic for its continuous innovation that offers both practical benefits and emotional value in a homogenized market.

## Key Insights
1.  **PC Gaming on Mobile is Here**: The 8E5 chip is powerful enough to run translated DX11 PC games smoothly at 30-40 fps at 720p resolution, marking a significant breakthrough for mobile gaming.
2.  **Peak Performance Requires Tuning**: The 8E5 has extremely high power consumption. To get the best gaming results, one cannot simply max out performance mode; it requires manually adjusting CPU and GPU frequencies for different games to balance power and heat.
3.  **Innovation That's Both Practical and Cool**: Red Magic continues to explore the gaming phone niche with features like the under-screen camera and this year's liquid cooling channel, which provide "emotional value" while also being genuinely useful.
4.  **Clear Differentiation Between Models**: The Pro version focuses on maximum endurance with its huge 8000mAh battery, while the Pro+ offers the new liquid cooling tech alongside faster wired and wireless charging.
5.  **Addressing Past Shortcomings**: This generation includes upgrades like an IPX8 waterproof fan and a new touch IC, aiming to fix previously criticized issues and improve everyday reliability.

## Notable Moments
- **Visible "Liquid Cooling"**: The transparent cooling channel on the back of the Pro+, where you can clearly see the liquid (and bubbles) flowing, is a major visual highlight.
- **Conquering *Black Myth: Wukong***: Successfully running *Black Myth: Wukong* at nearly 30 fps on the phone, which is described as a "qualitative leap" compared to the 10-ish fps they achieved six months prior.
- **Sky-High Power Consumption**: While running *Expedition 33*, the phone's total power draw peaked at over 18W, showcasing the incredible performance output of the 8E5 chip.
