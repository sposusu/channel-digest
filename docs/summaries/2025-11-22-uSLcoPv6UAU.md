# Is This How it Feels?!...

**Channel:** Hera - Age of Empires 2
**Published:** 2025-11-22
**Video:** [Watch on YouTube](https://youtube.com/watch?v=uSLcoPv6UAU)

**What happens**: A gamer effortlessly destroys a weak "65 HP gate" and then has a moment of realization, wondering if this is how their overpowered strength feels to their opponents.
**The point**: The joke is the gamer's sudden, slightly delayed empathy for how frustrating it must be to play against them.
