# 紅魔 11 Pro 系列上手評測：液冷散熱與 PC 遊戲實測

## 重點摘要
紅魔 11 Pro 系列搭載最新的 8 Elite Gen 5 處理器，性能強悍。Pro+ 款引入了創新的「液態冷卻」均熱板，而 Pro 款則配備了驚人的 8000mAh 大電池。實測證明，新機能夠更流暢地轉譯運行《黑神話：悟空》等 PC 大作，展現了其在極限性能上的巨大潛力。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：影片開箱了紅魔 11 Pro 與 11 Pro+ 兩款新機。最引人注目的是 Pro+ 背部中央的透明環，可以看到內部流動的液體，這是其首創的「水冷」均熱系統。同時介紹了風扇防水、螢幕觸控等升級點。
- **中段**：影片的核心是極限性能測試。由於傳統手遊已無挑戰性，團隊直接使用 PC 遊戲轉譯來壓榨 8E5 晶片的性能。他們測試了《戰神 4》，能以 40-50 幀流暢運行。接著挑戰了高難度的《黑神話：悟空》，幀率從半年前的 10 幾幀提升到 20-30 幀，成功擊敗 Boss，實現了「質的飛躍」。測試還表明，針對不同遊戲進行手動降頻調校，是發揮最佳性能的關鍵。
- **結尾**：結論是，8E5 晶片的性能強大，在紅魔優秀的散熱基礎上，以 30 幀為目標運行 PC 遊戲已成為可能。影片讚揚了紅魔在同質化的手機市場中，持續推出螢幕下鏡頭、散熱風扇、液冷系統等兼具實用與情感價值的創新。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **8E5 晶片性能飛躍**：新一代處理器極大提升了手機運行 PC 轉譯遊戲的能力，使《戰神》等大作變得流暢可玩，《黑神話：悟空》也從「能跑」變成了「勉強可玩」。
2.  **創新液冷均熱**：Pro+ 的「水冷」系統本質是液態均熱板，透過液體流動將熱量均勻分佈，雖然只帶來約 2 度的降溫，但視覺效果酷炫，為用戶帶來了情感價值。
3.  **精準的性能調校**：要發揮 8E5 的極致性能，不能無腦開啟「毀滅者模式」。針對不同遊戲，需要手動限制 CPU 或 GPU 的頻率，以平衡功耗與散熱，從而獲得最穩定的幀率。
4.  **差異化產品策略**：Pro 和 Pro+ 提供了明確的選擇：Pro 擁有 8000mAh 的超大電池主打續航，Pro+ 則以液冷系統、120W 快充和無線充電主打極致體驗。
5.  **遊戲手機的探索精神**：紅魔持續在遊戲手機這個獨特領域進行探索，從螢幕下鏡頭到主動散熱風扇，再到這次的液冷系統，不斷推出既實用又有趣的「黑科技」。

## 精彩時刻
- **流動的「水冷」**：Pro+ 背蓋下清晰可見的液體和氣泡流動，視覺衝擊力十足，是科技與美學的結合。
- **挑戰《黑神話：悟空》**：成功以超過 20 幀的表現擊敗「銀虎」Boss，證明了相比前代產品有「質的飛躍」。
- **功耗高達 18W**：在運行《Expedition 33》時，整機功耗一度超過 18W，展現了 8E5 晶片恐怖的性能極限。
- **「實用的噱頭」**：評測者稱讚紅魔的創新是「實用的噱頭」，既能帶來情感價值，也對遊戲體驗有實際幫助。

# Red Magic 11 Pro Series Hands-On Review: Liquid Cooling & PC Gaming Test

## TL;DR
The Red Magic 11 Pro series, powered by the latest 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, delivers formidable performance. The Pro+ model introduces an innovative "liquid cooling" heat spreader, while the Pro model boasts a massive 8000mAh battery. Testing shows the new phones can run PC games like *Black Myth: Wukong* more smoothly via emulation, showcasing their immense potential in extreme performance.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: The video unboxes the new Red Magic 11 Pro and 11 Pro+. The most eye-catching feature is the transparent ring on the back of the Pro+, revealing flowing liquid inside—its debut "water cooling" heat-spreading system. Other upgrades like the waterproof fan and improved screen touch controls are also introduced.
- **Middle**: The core of the video is the extreme performance test. Since traditional mobile games are no longer a challenge, the team uses PC game emulation to push the 8E5 chip to its limits. They test *God of War 4*, which runs smoothly at 40-50 fps. They then tackle the demanding *Black Myth: Wukong*, where the frame rate improves from 10+ fps six months ago to 20-30 fps, successfully defeating a boss and marking a "qualitative leap." The test also reveals that manually tuning clock speeds for different games is key to achieving optimal performance.
- **End**: The conclusion is that the 8E5 chip is powerful enough to make running PC games at a 30 fps target feasible, especially on a well-cooled phone like the Red Magic. The video praises Red Magic for its continuous innovation with practical and emotionally valuable features (under-screen camera, cooling fan, liquid system) in a homogenized smartphone market.

## Key Insights
1.  **8E5 Chip Performance Leap**: The new processor significantly enhances the phone's ability to run emulated PC games, making major titles like *God of War* smooth and playable, while turning *Black Myth: Wukong* from "runnable" to "barely playable."
2.  **Innovative Liquid Heat Spreader**: The "water cooling" on the Pro+ is essentially a liquid heat spreader that distributes heat evenly via fluid flow. While only providing a ~2-degree temperature drop, its cool visual effect adds emotional value for users.
3.  **Precision Performance Tuning**: To unleash the 8E5's full power, simply enabling "Destroyer Mode" isn't enough. Achieving the most stable frame rate requires manually limiting the CPU or GPU frequency on a per-game basis to balance power consumption and heat.
4.  **Differentiated Product Strategy**: The Pro and Pro+ offer clear choices: the Pro focuses on endurance with a huge 8000mAh battery, while the Pro+ targets the ultimate experience with its liquid cooling system, 120W fast charging, and wireless charging.
5.  **The Spirit of Gaming Phone Exploration**: Red Magic continues to explore the unique niche of gaming phones, consistently introducing "black technology" that is both practical and fun, from under-screen cameras and active cooling fans to this generation's liquid cooling.

## Notable Moments
- **Flowing "Water Cooling"**: The clearly visible liquid and bubbles flowing under the Pro+'s back cover create a strong visual impact, blending technology with aesthetics.
- **Tackling *Black Myth: Wukong***: Successfully defeating the "Yinhu" boss at over 20 fps, demonstrating a "qualitative leap" in performance compared to the previous generation.
- **Hitting 18W of Power**: While running *Expedition 33*, the phone's total power consumption peaked at over 18W, showcasing the incredible performance ceiling of the 8E5 chip.
- **"Practical Gimmicks"**: The reviewer praises Red Magic's innovations as "practical gimmicks" that provide both emotional value and real benefits to the gaming experience.
