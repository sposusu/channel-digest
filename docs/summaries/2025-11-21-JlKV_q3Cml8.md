# 世紀帝國2：1v3 伐木挑戰賽

## 重點摘要
頂尖玩家 Hera 在一場 1v3 對抗初學者的比賽中，放棄了傳統的速攻打法，轉而執行一個瘋狂的「支線任務」：砍光對手半場的所有樹木。這個以資源剝奪為核心的奇特策略最終奏效，讓他贏得了一場極具娛樂性的勝利。

## 故事大綱
- **開場**：Hera (排名前10) 對上三位 ELO 較低的玩家 (約 600-850)。他意識到如果全力以赴，比賽會一面倒，因此決定玩得「有趣」一點。他以快速封建時代的輕騎兵開局，進行了短暫的騷擾。
- **中段**：Hera 確立了他的主要目標：使用攻城武器（投石車/火箭炮）砍伐對手地圖上的所有樹木。他將大部分時間和資源都投入到這個漫長而細緻的伐木工作中，同時防禦對手的零星攻擊。他甚至在過程中發現了用攻城武器高效伐木的新技巧，並將其比喻為一集「鮑伯·魯斯繪畫教學」。
- **結尾**：伐木策略大獲成功。儘管對手集結了龐大的軍隊，但他們最終耗盡了所有木材，無法建造新的農田、建築或補充兵力。Hera 建造了一道「長城」石牆，徹底封鎖了最後的樹木，透過資源扼殺贏得了比賽。對手因無木可用而被迫投降。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **木材的終極價值**：在遊戲後期，木材對於維持經濟和軍隊的重要性，有時甚至超過黃金。
2.  **非常規的勝利條件**：跳脫框架思考，設定如「資源剝奪」這樣的創意勝利條件，也能出奇制勝。
3.  **樂趣大於勝利**：給自己設定個人挑戰，享受遊戲過程，比單純追求快速勝利更有趣、更令人難忘。
4.  **新手玩家的潛力**：初學者雖然前期較弱，但若給予足夠時間發展，他們在遊戲後期的經濟和軍事實力不容小覷。
5.  **戰略性耐心**：Hera 花了極長的時間執行他的伐木計畫，證明了耐心和專注於一個看似荒謬的長期目標，也能帶來最終的勝利。

## 精彩時刻
- Hera 決定執行「砍光所有樹木」這個瘋狂的支線任務。
- 觀看攻城武器像除草機一樣，大片大片地清除森林，畫面極度療癒。
- Hera 剛說完「我看過有職業選手會誤傷自己的單位」，下一秒就用友軍砲火炸死了自己的投石車。
- 遊戲尾聲，對手村民因無樹可砍而四處遊蕩，經濟完全停擺的畫面。
- Hera 戲謔地指控對手作弊，並在最後用石牆將對手與最後的木材隔開，稱之為他的「萬里長城」。

# Age of Empires 2: The 1v3 Deforestation Challenge

## TL;DR
In a 1v3 match against beginners, top player Hera forgoes a standard victory to pursue a wild "side quest": chopping down every tree on his opponents' half of the map. This creative strategy of resource denial ultimately leads to a unique and highly entertaining win.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning**: Hera (a top 10 player) is matched against three much lower-ranked players (around 600-850 ELO). Realizing a serious game would be a stomp, he decides to have fun, opening with a light scout rush for some early pressure.
- **Middle**: Hera commits to his main objective: using siege units (Onagers/Rocket Carts) to cut down all the wood on the enemy's territory. He dedicates the majority of the game to this long, meticulous process, comparing it to a "Bob Ross" episode of cutting trees, all while fending off attacks.
- **End**: The wood denial strategy pays off perfectly. The opponents, despite amassing a large army, run out of wood and cannot sustain their economy—no new farms, buildings, or units. Hera builds a "Great Wall" to seal off the last remaining trees, securing victory by resource starvation as the opponents are forced to resign.

## Key Insights
1.  **The Ultimate Value of Wood**: In the late game, wood can be a more critical resource than gold for sustaining an economy and army.
2.  **Unconventional Win Conditions**: Thinking outside the box and setting creative goals, like total resource denial, can be surprisingly effective.
3.  **Fun Over Pure Victory**: Setting personal challenges and enjoying the process can be more memorable and rewarding than just winning as quickly as possible.
4.  **The Potential of New Players**: Beginners often struggle in the early game but can become formidable in the late game if left to build up their economy.
5.  **Strategic Patience**: Hera's dedication to his long-term, seemingly absurd wood-cutting plan demonstrates that patience and focus can lead to victory.

## Notable Moments
- Hera's decision to embark on the "side quest" to cut all the enemy's wood.
- The incredibly satisfying visuals of siege weapons clearing massive swathes of forest.
- Hera ironically killing his own siege units with friendly fire moments after talking about how other pros make that mistake.
- The final moments where the enemy villagers are idle because there is literally no wood left for them to chop.
- Hera jokingly accusing his opponent of cheating and later building his own "Great Wall of China" to block them from the last trees.
