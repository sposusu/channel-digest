# 我們能在 2025 年用 2000 美元組裝一台猛獸級電競電腦嗎？

## 重點摘要
儘管零組件價格令人沮喪地高昂，但這部影片證明，只要做出聰明、注重價值的選擇（例如使用上一代的 CPU），你仍然可以用大約 2000 美元組裝出一台性能強大的 4K 遊戲電腦。這個組裝指南展示了整個過程，並證明 DIY 仍然是獲得卓越性能的絕佳選擇。

## 故事大綱
- **開場：** 影片主持人承認目前組裝電腦的成本很高，但他設定目標要證明 2000 美元仍然可以組裝出一台出色的遊戲主機。他介紹了贊助商 Seasonic，並開始挑選零件。
- **中段：** 影片逐步展示了零件的選擇和詳細的組裝過程。關鍵的省錢策略是選擇上一代的 AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D CPU，搭配一張性價比高的主機板和一個出奇便宜又高效的 Thermalright 散熱器。在預算限制下，NVIDIA RTX 4070 Ti 被選為最實際的顯示卡。指南涵蓋了 CPU、記憶體、SSD 和 GPU 的安裝，並提供了理線和故障排除的技巧。
- **結尾：** 電腦在第一次嘗試時就成功開機。隨後，在《Anno》新作、《電馭叛客 2077》和《CS2》等遊戲中以 4K 解析度進行了基準測試，展現了令人印象深刻的性能，且運行時既安靜又低溫。結論是，自己動手組裝電腦仍然是讓錢花得最物超所值的方式。

## 關鍵見解
1.  **聰明消費勝過追新：** 選擇像 7800X3D 這樣的頂級上一代 CPU，可以在實際遊戲性能損失極小的情況下，為你省下一大筆錢。
2.  **價值是王道：** 你不一定需要最昂貴的零件。尋找特價品和像 Thermalright 散熱器這樣高價值的零件，是成功管理預算的關鍵。
3.  **系統平衡至關重要：** 在有限的預算內，打造一個各方面均衡的系統，比把所有錢都砸在頂級 GPU 上卻犧牲其他零件要好得多，這樣能帶來更全面的使用體驗。
4.  **DIY 的優勢：** 與同價位的品牌機相比，自己組裝可以在機殼、電源供應器和 SSD 功能（如 DRAM 快取）等細節上獲得更高品質的零件。
5.  **GPU 市場的現實：** 在這個特定的價位（約 800 美元），即使定價不甚理想，NVIDIA 的產品因其卓越的光線追蹤和升頻技術（DLSS）而具有競爭優勢。

# Can We Build a BEAST Gaming PC for $2000 in 2025?

## TL;DR
Despite discouragingly high component prices, this video demonstrates that you can still build a powerful 4K-capable gaming PC for around $2000. By making smart, value-focused choices like using a last-gen CPU, the build achieves excellent performance and proves DIY is still a fantastic option.

## Story Flow
- **Beginning:** The host acknowledges the high cost of PC parts but sets out to prove a great $2000 gaming PC is still achievable, with Seasonic sponsoring the build.
- **Middle:** The video walks through the component selection and assembly process. Key choices include a last-gen AMD 7800X3D CPU to save money, a value-packed motherboard, a surprisingly cheap yet effective Thermalright cooler, and an NVIDIA 4070 Ti as the most practical GPU for the budget. The guide covers the installation of the CPU, RAM, SSD, and GPU, with tips for cable management and troubleshooting.
- **End:** The PC boots successfully on the first try. It's then benchmarked in games like a new *Anno*, *Cyberpunk 2077*, and *CS2* at 4K, demonstrating impressive, quiet, and cool performance. The conclusion is that building your own PC remains the best way to get maximum value for your money.

## Key Insights
1.  **Smart Savings Over Latest-Gen:** Opting for a top-tier last-generation CPU (like the 7800X3D) can save a significant amount of money with negligible real-world gaming performance loss.
2.  **Value is King:** You don't always need the most expensive components. Seeking out sales and high-value parts like the Thermalright cooler is crucial for budget management.
3.  **System Balance is Key:** In a limited budget, creating a well-balanced system is better than splurging on a top-tier GPU while compromising on other components, leading to a better overall user experience.
4.  **The DIY Advantage:** Compared to similarly priced pre-built systems, building it yourself allows for better quality components in areas like the case, power supply, and SSD features (e.g., DRAM cache).
5.  **GPU Market Realities:** At this specific price point (around $800), NVIDIA's offerings hold a competitive edge due to superior ray tracing and upscaling (DLSS) technology, even if the pricing isn't ideal.
